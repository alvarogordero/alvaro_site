Who Is Alvaro Gordero?


Alvaro Gordero, (born in Spain, December, 16, 1991) is an architect, and entrepreneur.

Alvaro and his brother Gonzalo Gordero are founders of ITF, a fitness club in Shanghai.

During their university years they co-founded several startups that didn't make it through.

Some of them were:

  • 2012 - DisLexia. It was an e-learning app for kids. It won a first prize in a national entrepreneurship contest.
  • 2015 - Underwater winery. It didn't get the investment to launch it.
  • 2017 - MyBike . Just landed in Shanghai Gonzalo saw something beside their apartment. It was a small company called Mobike, not very popular for that time (later Alvaro and Gonzalo rented Mobike's first office to expand ITF). Gonzalo saw the potential and both started developing the product. They introduced the first bike-sharing without station in Europe. Finally their chinese competitors, Ofo and Mobike were growing so fast, and the investors started losing the interest.

In 2017 Alvaro and his brother Gonzalo founded ITF. ITF is working since 2017 and has created a huge community in the heart of Yangpu district, Shanghai, the new technology center of China. Surrounded by some of the best Universities in China, like Fudan University or Tongji University. ITF is not only a business business but also a community that mixes people from all around the world, and help them to improve their lifes.


Currently Alvaro is working in new projects that will see the light soon.
