The first B2B in the Bike-Sharing without station market.


Back to 2017, just landed in Shanghai Gonzalo saw something beside their apartment. It was a small company called Mobike, it wasn't very popular for that time (later Alvaro and Gonzalo rented Mobike's first office to expand ITF). Gonzalo saw the potential and both started developing the product. They introduced the first bike sharing system in Europe. Finally their Chinese competitors, Ofo and Mobike were growing so fast, and the potential investors started losing the interest.



This was the first bike with Smartlock in Europe. At this time the prototype was perfectly working and they were looking for VC in Barcelona.

1.1. IDEA

One app to manage thousands of small business.


What was their company going to make?

A software for the management of all small companies of bike-sharing without station merged into the same brand. MyBIke will provide to bicycle rental companies a management service and hardware. This centralized management service allows you to attract more customers since it allows users to enjoy the system further than their cities. MyBike manages the deposits giving international coverage the user. How will we make money? MyBike charges a fee to the companies’ franchisee for each transaction made to rent a bike. Moreover, with advertisement.

What is bike-sharing without station?

Is an innovative mobility program to reach the maximum flexibility of bike-sharing system.Also is a low-cost system which allows you to access to bicycles located each 100 meters in the city and park it in your destination.

Features:

  • Free parking place: Park your bicycle wherever you want in the city.
  • Smartbikes: With GPS System, powered by solar and dynamo systems.
  • Instant payment: Recharge your wallet easily, and scan the QR code to unlock the bike. After that, you will have to pay each 30 minutes, the app will do it automatically if you are riding the bike.

1.3. OPORTUNITY


Problem:

  • MyBike solve the problem of the last Km in the cities, routes which are too short to go by taxi or bus, but long to go on foot.

Solution:

  • Interurban transport net created by smart bicycles structure. This net formed by MyBike bicycle, will be controlled by app. Find the bicycle, scan the code, unblock the Smartlock and start riding. With this system always will have available a bicycle each 100 meters.

2.1 BUSINESS MODEL


B2B2C

It is presented a hierarchical model in which MyBike, It will provide a joint image to the various companies that currently operate in the market. The purpose is to promote an international brand, with greater capacity for customer acquisition more competitive and which encourages customer. Moving internationally and enjoy the service will not be a problem with centralized system.


What do we provide?

  • A joint brand.
  • Back-end management software.
  • App & Smart Lock
  • Bicycles in case that your current fleet does not meet the requirements for the city.



How does the business model work?

MyBike will be responsible, as if Uber were it, where the drivers were the bicycle rental companies, to provide a management service. This centralized management service allows you to attract more customers since it allows users to enjoy the system further than their cities.

MyBike charges a fee for each transaction made to rent a bike.

MyBike system manages the deposits giving international coverage to the user.


2.2. MARKET:


The potential market will be all the companies in the developed cities with the right urban planning of the city, suitable for the use of the bicycle and enough mass of consumers adapted to mobile payments.
