In 2017 Alvaro and his brother Gonzalo founded ITF, a fitness club that aims to change people's life. ITF is working since 2017 and has created a huge community in the heart of Yangpu district, Shanghai, the new technology center of China. In The Factory, 缘分

How did they start? The answer to this question could be a chinese concept, 缘分 yuánfèn. Yuanfen is the fate or chance that brings people together. It can also be translated as "destiny, conditioned by one's past".


In 2017 just a few months after landing in Shanghai, China, with a population of more than 20 million people, Alvaro and Gonzalo met by accident a local Chinese. This local Chinese studied in the same University as they did in Spain 15 years ago, and also used to work selling DVDs on the streets of their hometown to pay his bills. At this moment, the man who used to sell DVDs on the streets, became a successful businessman and had as a hobby working out. Joined by the same passion, Liu Ning (the Chinese businessman), asked for some advices and later on they will become partners.

The Great Jiangwan

Certainly the factory was a magic place but unquestionably, it wasn't the right place to run a business. It was very hard for the members to find the area and it was in poor conditions. Following this, Gonzalo was committed from the first day to find the right place.

Six months later Gonzalo found a place. This place was The Great Jiangwan Stadium. It was very small and dark, very difficult to make the business profitable because of the dimensions of the space weren't enough to fit the members in the class, but Gonzalo sure of himself convinced Alvaro to move to that place.
