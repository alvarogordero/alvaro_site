DisLexia

During the summer break of 2012 Alvaro and his brother Gonzalo Gordero and a friend decided to start a new project. Looking on the internet they found a national contest, and they went for it. This summer they created DisLexia. It was an e-learning app for kids. This app won the first prize. This contest was created by Calidad Pascual (formerly known as Grupo Leche Pascual) one of the biggest companies in Spain.
